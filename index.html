About

Senior Spring is a product design studio dedicated to exploring new forms, functions, materials and meanings for products. For our clients, this means new opportunities for product innovation and brand development.

Project diversity is central to our search for what is new. A dynamic cross-fertilization amongst technology, consumer, tabletop and furniture development programs provides unique perspective and a better understanding of how design contributes to a successful product.

In pursuit of product excellence, we combine research, devotion to functionality and artful form-giving with a keen cultural and commercial awareness. Supporting these essential strengths is an experienced, reasoned approach to engineering and manufacturing. The net result of our process is exceptional new products with enduring intrinsic value.

Envison

Vision couples discovery with resolution, serving as a catalyst for change. Charting a new course requires that we identify the true nature of a product opportunity. At the beginning, it's not what we design, but why we're designing the product. Our work provides insight and context for the product development process. We examine the product user within the broader ecosystem, we ask questions, and we do product development research in search of deeper meaning that leads to heightened experience.

Build

The best thinking comes from people who are accountable for, and part of, the making of the thing. Insights, vision, and intent are rendered powerless without proper and thorough execution. Creative minds become more innovative when they physically construct the things that they envision because the art of building means that we learn from doing. At Speck Design we are focused on making things, solving practical problems, creating wonderful experiences, and making useful things beautiful.

Transform

Design has the power to transform thinking, behavior, experiences, and outcomes. Our commitment to product transformation means we are accountable and involved from the initial briefing, through creation, to the final evaluation of products that we design. At the outset of every product development project, we collaborate with our clients to clearly define the desired transformations. The design path is guided by these objectives, and we hold ourselves accountable for achieving them.
