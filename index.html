Kahasi

ecoSmart Habitat is designed to be a completely self-sufficient and self-sustaining single family home. Operating solely on renewable resources to support a family of four or more with a comfortable, healthy and safe lifestyle.

It generates, stores and distributes power, water, food and recycles/disposes waste generated by its occupants. Fully automated operation, from the indoor climate and lighting control to the micro agricultural system built within the home and more.
